Synopsis

The objective for this assignment is to predict the manner in which the subjects did the exercise. The quality of the exercise is represented by the “classe” variable in the training set. Other variables are used as predictor variables.You should create a report describing how you built your model, how you used cross validation, what you think the expected out of sample error is, and why you made the choices you did. You will also use your prediction model to predict 20 different test cases.
